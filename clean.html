Cleaning :-

Here’s what Pratyush has made a commitment to do before every stay :-


1.Sanitise surfaces :-
Sanitise every high-touch surface, down to the doorknob.

2.Use approved products :-
Use cleaners approved by health experts, such as disinfectants with 70% alcohol or higher

3.Thoroughly clean :-
Refer to the extensive checklists while cleaning room by room

4.Wear a mask and gloves :-
Help avoid cross-contamination by wearing a mask and gloves.

5.Wash all linen :-
Wash linen at a high heat setting.

6.Follow all other local guidance :-
Comply with local laws, including any additional safety or cleaning guidelines
